Project on Food Wastage

Food wastage is a significant global issue that affects not only our environment but also people's lives. Our project aims to raise awareness about this problem and encourage responsible consumption and food management practices.

Objectives

  • Reduce food wastage at individual and household levels
  • Collaborate with local restaurants and grocery stores to minimize wastage in the supply chain
  • Educate the community about the environmental and social impact of food wastage
  • Promote sustainable food practices, such as composting and food donation

Key Features

  1. Food Waste Tracker: Keep track of the amount of food wasted by individuals or households. This feature allows users to set goals and monitor their progress in reducing food wastage.
  2. Community Forum: A platform for users to share tips, recipes, and success stories related to minimizing food wastage. It fosters a sense of community and encourages knowledge sharing.
  3. Donation Network: Connects users with local food banks and charitable organizations to facilitate food donation and redistribution.
  4. Information Hub: Provides educational resources, articles, and infographics to raise awareness and inform users about the impact of food wastage on the environment and society.
